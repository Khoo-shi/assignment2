Discover Beautiful Breeds

Siamese

Known for blue eyes and chatty personality. Very social and elegant.

Persian

Long, fluffy fur and a calm nature. Needs regular grooming.

Maine Coon

Big, friendly, and playful. One of the largest cat breeds.

Bengal

Spotted coat like a wild cat. Active and loves climbing.

Ragdoll

Very relaxed and affectionate. Often goes limp when picked up.

British Shorthair

Round face, dense coat, and calm temperament. Great for indoors.
